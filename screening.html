Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I have vein disease?

If you are suffering with strange and usual symptoms such as burning, itchy, or heavy legs, varicose veins, or other legs symptoms then it may be chronic venous insufficiency. Schedule your free screening to determine if you need a leg ultrasound with one of our vein doctors.

How do you diagnose vein disease?

A simple, quick, and painless ultrasound will help diagnose vein disease as the cause to your symptoms.

Is vein disease covered by insurance?

Vein disease is a medical condition covered by insurance. We'll help you find out if you qualify for 100% coverage.

What are the signs of vein disease?

Here are the most common signs of vein disease


  • Varicose Veins
  • Swelling of the feet, ankles or legs
  • Cramping
  • Aching or throbbing
  • Pain that worsens the longer you are on your feet
  • Tightness around the calves or ankles
  • Itchy legs
  • Weakness or a sensation of heaviness in the legs
  • Skin discoloration or changes in the skin texture
  • Restless leg syndrome
  • Leg ulcers that are slow to heal
